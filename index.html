Welcome!

High-Performance Metallic Structure (HPMS) Research Group is led by Dr. Ke Jiang at University of Canterbury, New Zealand. Our group conducts research into high-performance metallic structures, with a particular emphasis on:

• High-performance connections and members

• Anti-seismic devices

• Post-fire Behaviour of steel and composite structures

• Artificial Intelligence in Structures
